For installation of grid connected rooftop solar (RTS) project under PM - Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana

This agreement is executed on 19-06-2025 for design, supply, installation, commissioning and 5-year comprehensive maintenance of RTS project/system along with warranty under PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana.

Parties

First Party (Consumer / Purchaser / Owner): DANIYALBHAI DHULAJI BALEVA having address at PO: NANA …

Second Party (Vendor): SHIV … RADI

The First Party wishes to install a Grid Connected Rooftop Solar Plant on their building under PM Surya Ghar Muft Bijli Yojana at SHOP NO.04, MYPUR TALUKA LODA DIST. JAMNAGAR.

The Second Party has verified availability of appropriate roof and found it feasible to install a grid-connected Rooftop Solar plant and is willing to design, supply, install, test, commission and carry out operation & maintenance of the Rooftop solar plant for 5 years.

Guidelines for PM Surya Ghar: Muft Bijli Yojana

  1. Submission of online application at National Portal for installation of RTS project/system, application for net metering, system inspection, and upload of relevant documents.
  2. Provide secure storage of materials delivered at premises.
  3. Provide access to roof during installation, operation & maintenance, meter reading, etc.
  4. Provide electricity during installation and water for cleaning of panels.
  5. Report malfunctioning of plant to Vendor during warranty period.
  6. Pay agreed amount as per payment schedule, including any additional amount for extra work depending on building condition.
  7. Vendor must follow all standards and safety guidelines prescribed by MNRE/State.

Scope of Vendor

  • Site Survey: Survey, feasibility study of roof, roof strength, shadow-free area, customization if required. Vendor prepares estimate and separate invoice for additional work.
  • Design & Engineering: Plant design, drawings, component selection as per DISCOM/SERC/MNRE standards.
  • Modules & Inverter: Must be manufactured in India, conform to MNRE standards.
  • Procurement & Supply: Materials should comply with BIS/IEC standards and MNRE subsidy rules.
  • Installation & Civil Work: Civil structure, wiring, electrical work as per BIS standards.
  • Documentation: Catalogues, warranty certificates, BIS certificates, layout drawings, test reports, etc. to be provided for consumer upload.
  • Project Completion Report (PCR): To be signed and uploaded on National Portal.
  • Warranty: Full system warranty 5 years; component warranty as per manufacturer.
  • Net Meter & Grid Connectivity: Vendor arranges net meter supply/procurement, testing, approval.
  • Testing & Commissioning: Vendor must be present during DISCOM commissioning.
  • Operation & Maintenance: 5 years comprehensive O&M, overhauling, wear & tear checks, consumer training for cleaning/maintenance.
  • Insurance: Vendor responsible for insurance of material until commissioning.
  • Applicable Standards: Vendor must comply with MNRE and State technical standards.
  • Cost & Payment Terms: To be mutually decided. Consumer may opt for milestone-based payment.
  • Disputes: Any dispute between consumer & vendor to be mutually resolved; MNRE/DISCOM not liable.
  • Subsidy/Project Documents: Vendor to provide required documents to consumer for subsidy claim.
  • Performance Ratio (PR): Minimum 75% PR at time of commissioning. Vendor to provide irradiation sensor (NABL/International certified). PR to be maintained during 5-year warranty.

Disclaimer

This agreement is between vendor and consumer. Any dispute related to the same shall not involve any third party including MNRE and Distribution Utilities.
